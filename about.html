MINERBOT

The MinerBot is a chatbot that can answer your questions about mining industry acts, rules, and regulations. It is powered by a large language model that has been trained on a massive dataset of text and code. The MinerBot is still under development, but it has learned to perform many kinds of tasks, including:

  • Answering questions about mining industry acts, rules, and regulations
  • Providing information on mining industry best practices
  • Helping users to find relevant resources on mining industry regulations

The MinerBot is a valuable tool for anyone who works in the mining industry or who is interested in learning more about mining industry regulations. It is easy to use and can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection.
